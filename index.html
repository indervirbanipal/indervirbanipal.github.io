About Me

            

As a part of Watson Discovery team at IBM Watson, I build AI-powered search engine capabilities to understand complex business documents. I am a member of IBM's Invention Development Team (IDT) which is responsible for evaluating inventions and mentor inventors in the areas of Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning. Previously, I have contributed to Watson Assistant, Watson Labs and Big Data Lab at UT Dallas. Before my masters in Computer Science from The University of Texas at Dallas, I completed my bachelors in Electrical & Electronics Engineering from Birla Institute of Technology & Science, Pilani.
